Our History

Asia Gardens Hotel & Thai Spa was established in 2005, inspired by a passion for Asian aesthetics and Mediterranean warmth.
The founders envisioned a serene sanctuary where guests could experience luxury intertwined with tranquil gardens and authentic Asian hospitality. Over the years, it has grown to become a beloved destination for those seeking a blend of relaxation, culture, and culinary delights.

Our Team

At Asia Gardens Hotel & Thai Spa, our team is the soul of our sanctuary. United by a passion for Asian aesthetics and Mediterranean hospitality, we bring together skilled professionals from diverse backgrounds—spa therapists, culinary artists, guest service experts—each committed to delivering excellence with grace. Our shared vision is to create a haven where guests feel truly cared for, surrounded by beauty, tranquility, and authentic cultural warmth.
