Dining

Experience exquisite dining at Asia Garden Hotel, where culinary excellence meets breathtaking ambiance. Savor a diverse array of gourmet dishes crafted by our world-class chefs, using the freshest ingredients to tantalize your taste buds. Whether you're indulging in a romantic dinner for two or celebrating a special occasion with loved ones, our elegant restaurants and attentive service ensure an unforgettable dining experience. Join us and embark on a gastronomic journey that delights all your senses.

Date: Every evening in October and November

Time:18:00 - 23:00
