Our History

Asia Gardens Hotel & Thai Spa was established in 2005, inspired by a passion for Asian aesthetics and Mediterranean warmth.
The founders envisioned a serene sanctuary where guests could experience luxury intertwined with tranquil gardens and authentic Asian hospitality. Over the years, it has grown to become a beloved destination for those seeking a blend of relaxation, culture, and culinary delights.
